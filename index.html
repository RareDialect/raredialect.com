Each run in Fugue Shot takes place in a shifting dreamscape filled with dangerous encounters known as the Omens, ranging from strategic puzzles to fast-paced action arenas. Face these challenges and collect Memories that alter properties across all Omens, reshaping the dream and the way you play. Can you overcome the unstable terrain of this liminal world, or will it consume you?

  • Face any of 10 unique Omens, each with their own gameplay style and variety
  • Collect over 150 Memories to alter the dreamscape and your playstyle
  • Chip away at over 100 permanent unlocks by completing challenges
  • Enjoy a beautiful, era-ambiguous art style and meditative dynamic soundtrack as you explore the dream world
